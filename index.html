Pūrṇam

A Journal of Indian Knowledge Systems

Pūrṇam is a peer reviewed, diamond access, paying, multi-disciplinary, multi-lingual Journal of Indian Knowledge Systems with the following key features

Periodicity
Bi-Annual journal with additional special issues as and when announced
Multi-linguality
The journal accepts papers in vernacular languages and English. Currently Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada & Sanskrit language papers are accepted. Other language writers may write to the publishers to explore
Review
Blinded peer-reviewed
Publisher
Bharat Vikas Sangam
Mode of publication
Print and e-versions
